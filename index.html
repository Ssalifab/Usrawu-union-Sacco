About USRAWU Multipurpose Co-operative Society

To prosper through unity and innovation

USRAWU Multipurpose Co-operative Society Limited is a fully registered member-owned financial cooperative established to provide affordable financial services to scientists, researchers, and allied workers across Uganda.

Our Vision

Transformed members' livelihoods through secure savings, affordable financial products and utilization of science and innovation for overall enhanced value of the cooperative.

Our Mission

To be a leading cooperative society in mobilisation of members and resources, internal policy implementation of scientific innovative projects, provision of affordable loans and monetary benefits to its members.

Our Administration

Dedicated leadership committed to serving our members with integrity and professionalism.

Board of Directors

The Board provides overall leadership and management of the SACCO and is accountable to the members. As the policy organ of the SACCO, it develops relevant policies approved by Annual General Meetings (AGMs) and organizes members' education and mobilisation activities.

Supervisory Committee

This Committee ensures that the Board operates within the Co-operative Societies Act, Regulations and By-laws of the SACCO and any other relevant laws. It ensures that resolutions passed by members during AGMs are duly and promptly implemented for the benefit of members, supervises the operations of the Board, and meets at least once quarterly.

Investment Committee

This Committee is responsible for advising the Board on feasible investment options and innovative products in line with members' needs. It is also responsible for development and review of the SACCO's investment policy.

Loans Committee

The Loans Committee is responsible for consideration for approval of loan requests, and follow-up on their full recovery. It is also responsible for development and review of the SACCO's loans policy.
